What is the drawback of declaring methods directly in JavaScript objects?

Encapsulation and Modularity: Lack of encapsulation and difficulty in accessing private variables or methods. Readability: Object literals can become cluttered and less readable, especially with longer or complex methods. Testing and Reusability: Methods are less reusable and harder to test independently. Prototypal Inheritance: Directly declared methods do not benefit from prototypal inheritance, potentially leading to higher memory usage. Code Maintenance: Refactoring and extending functionality can be more challenging and error-prone.
