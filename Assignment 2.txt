What is SEO?
- SEO stands for search engine optimization. 
- SEO is used to rank the website on top among the searches. 
  SEO depends upon different factors to rank the website like keywords research, by advertising, content etc.

What is Domain?
- Domain name refers to your website address.
  eg. www.google.com , www.facebook.com
  The .com is called the top level domain.
  The google is called the second level domain.
